# Staff Product Manager — Globex

Globex is hiring a Staff Product Manager to own our platform roadmap end to end.

## What you'll do
- Set the strategy for the core platform and align three engineering teams behind it.
- Partner with design and data science to ship measurable customer outcomes.
- Coach and mentor a small group of product managers.

## What we're looking for
- 8+ years in product, with 2+ years at staff level or leading a product area.
- Strong written communication and stakeholder management.
- A track record of shipping complex, cross-team initiatives.

## How we work
We value ownership, bias for action, and customer obsession.
